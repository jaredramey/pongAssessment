Thank you for reading the ReadMe File! Here you can read how to play the game and get a usefull hint to outplay your apponent!

(Helpfull tip: Your paddle is an alien space ship! Alien spaceships have tractor beams! If the ball is just a little behind your paddle then it will curve around and fling at your apponent!)

How to play:

Player 1 - 
Up:    W
Down:  S
Left:  A
Right: D

Player 2 -
Up:    Up Arrow   /\
Down:  Down Arrow \/
Left:  Left Arrow <--
Right: Right Arrow -->

During the game you can press the Backspace key at any time to return to the Main Menu!
